Projects

This section will outline some of the programming projects I've done over the past couple of years. I have used Javscript, HTML, CSS, Python, C, and Java for these projects! Click on the images if you want to see the Github repository with the code.

Project Description

Well.. this website!: Jun 2024-Jul 2024

This website has been coded from scratch using HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Building this website was a fun way to develop my coding skills and creativity and share more about myself.

Reversi: Apr 2024-Apr 2024

Using C, I developed and programmed the popular game of Reversi to be played between the user and the computer. I programmed and developed an algorithm that determines the best available move for the computer based on the moves made by the user. Validity and availability of user inputted moves were also taken into account.

Pacemaker Data Analyzer: Feb 2024-Feb 2024

At a hackathon, our team designed an application using Python that would be capable of analyzing data from a pacemaker, which in turn helps detect heart irregularities and the possibility of low battery levels in the pacemaker. Our team obtained pacemaker data from large datasets in order to analyze trends and get rid of possible outliers in our program. Additionally, we executed user interface testing to validate the app's usability and functionality of interactive elements on the application, such as buttons and menus.

Employee Management System: Feb 2023-Jun 2023

For my employee management system, I used Java to program back-end and Graphical User Interface for the application. I implemented a functional stack and queue system, using LIFO and FIFO principles, that was able to store the information of employees in a hash table data structure. System capabilities include adding, removing, and modifying employee profiles, as well as specifying employee attributes such as salary and experience.

President Card Game: Dec 2021-Jun 2022

I coded the popular card game President in Python. I designed and implemented game mechanics, such as shuffling cards, dealing cards, determining user turn order, and ranking players and cards. Additionally, I determined interactions between the cards placed by the players, and the outcomes of each card interaction in each scenario.

Music

Music! I love listening to music and enjoy discovering new music and artists to add to my favourites list. This section just talks about some of my favourite albums from some of my favourite artists. If you have not already listened to these albums, please give a listen, hopefully you will also enjoy them as much as I do! This section may change over time as my music taste evolves:)

good kid, m.A.A.d city

Arguably my favourite hip hop album of all time. The sounds, the production, the rapping, and the storytelling all come together to make one awesome album. I find it incredibly unique as to how the artist is able to tell a story throughout the album that delivers a powerful and meaningful message about finding yourself. I suggest this to anyone who wishes to get more into hip hop music!

The House Is Burning

This album is such a good listen from top to bottom. It has all types of tracks with different moods and vibes, such as chill, moody, and even upbeat, hype vibes. Great album to listen to, and I really like the creative cover art.:)

C U Girl

This one isn't an album, but it's one of of my favourite songs ever. It's only around 2 minutes long, but the production of this song gives a feel good and a warm vibe that makes it enjoyable.
